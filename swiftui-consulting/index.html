Why SwiftUI

  • Faster iteration cycles compared to UIKit, which means quicker time to market.
  • Native look and feel with less code, including automatic support for Dark Mode and Dynamic Type.
  • Single codebase that adapts to iPhone, iPad, Mac, and Apple Watch.
  • Modern concurrency with Swift and the Observation framework for clean, reactive data flow.

When I bring in UIKit

  • Complex collection view layouts that need UICollectionView compositional layout.
  • Camera and media capture workflows that need fine-grained AVFoundation control.
  • Legacy codebases where a gradual SwiftUI adoption strategy makes more sense.
  • Performance-critical screens where UIKit rendering gives measurable gains.
